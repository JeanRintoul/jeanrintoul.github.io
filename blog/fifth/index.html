The only way forward: the argument for man-machine interfaces.

PUBLISHED ON OCT 3, 2016 — THOUGHTS

The end of humanity and the rise of AI.

How should you respond to that which has surpassed you? Fight it, or join it.

The argument for man-machine interfaces.

Will you lose to that which has surpassed you?

It is the best thing to do. Recognition of a superior AI, then if you can’t beat them - join them. How though? Joining them is the only option, if you cannot destroy them. Right now most of humanity can’t even identify this is the problem, but instead is blaming foreigners taking their jobs, women joining the workforce, birth control and walls between countries. What a diversion from the actual problem?! What weak minded souls we are.

The problem is most of us are not as good as the best AI, and AI is evolving fast. It is faster than human evolution as we are trapped into our bodies.

We may not be able to compete, but, we may be able to join them if we work to meld ourselves into technology, including evolving our bodies. Surely there is remnant wisdom in the eons of biological evolution that will make for the strongest combined AI?

Technology is the enemy, not foreigners, women or african americans.
